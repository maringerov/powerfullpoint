About

Powerful Point is a an attempt to inspire change in the art of presenting. Going through the following pages you will be exposed to knowledge about the preparation, design, delivery and evaluation of a presentation. It is a constantly evolving project: new content will be added and old one - updated. This is a product of Sekwoia.

Team

Boyan Angelov and Marin Gerov are the team behind Powerful Point. Likeminded, yet with quite different backgrounds in microbiology and marketing, respectively, they share an ambition to change the way most people and organisations regard presentations. Their motto: "Always is the right moment!" Contact them here.

Book

Powerful Point is a book divided into four main sections, corresponding with the four stages of presenting: Preparation, Design, Delivery and Evaluation. It is available to you for free on this website. If you prefer to read it when you are off the grid, you can download it for Kindle or iBooks. Now, go on your way to presentation greatness!
